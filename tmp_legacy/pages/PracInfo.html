About our Medical Practice

This Medical Practice is an exciting place to work; we are an ambitious group of GPs. As a training practice we continuously reflect on our core vision and remain focused on providing quality care, always looking at ways to improve our patient’s experience. Like any successful team, we invest in the wellbeing of every single member, providing training, clinical and management supervision, making sure we recruit people who share our values. We have 4 GP Partners, a Salaried Doctor, 3 Nurses, 2 HCA’s and a small team of administration and reception staff.


Medical Research

General Practice Research Database

This Practice contributes data to the General Practice Rersearch Database (GPRD)which is run on a non-profit-making basis by the UK Medicines and Healthcare products Regulatory Agency (MHRA) in London. The information held on the database is used for a variety of medical and public health research purposes including:

  • Investigating side effects of medicines
  • Investigating causes of disease and medical disorders
