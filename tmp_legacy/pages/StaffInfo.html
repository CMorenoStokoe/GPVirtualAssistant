Our Practice Nurse Team are available by appointment each weekday morning and afternoon. They can also provide advice about dieting, giving up smoking, routine immunisations, travel and general health problems.

As part of our Primary Care Co-ordinator Team, Kay Confaloni, carries out visits to our elderly and housebound patients who are unable get to the surgery.

Other Clinical Staff allied to the Practice (but not Practice Employees:

Community Nursing (District Nurses)
The community nursing team will be happy to advise on the nursing care of the elderly, disabled and those recently discharged from hospital. To contact the District Nurses.

Health Visiting
The Health Visitors team is dedicated to good health promotion and disease prevention advice, particularly to parents and children under 5. To contact them please call & leave a message on the answerphone on 0300 4218358. They run a 'drop in' child welfare & baby weighing clinic at Elmbridge Childrens Centre, Windfall Way, Elmbridge, Gloucester on Wednesdays between 1.30and 2.30pm.

Mental Health Nurse
We have a member of the Primary Care Mental Health Team who holds a counselling session here at the surgery on a weekly basis.
Please note: appointments for this service are only made by referral from the doctors.

Midwife
Our community midwife looks after patients throughout pregnancy. In association with the health visitors the midwife runs parent craft classes. Anyone wishing to contact the midwife can either leave a message or make an appointment at the surgery.

Administration Staff

Medical Administration

Behind the scenes of any doctors’ surgery there are a team of people involved with the clerical and administrative needs of the practice. At Barnwood Medical Practice, we have a small dedicated team of people who are responsible for a wide range of tasks. These include coding medical events and conditions onto patients’ computerised medical records, summarising new patients’ records, scanning letters onto patients’ computerised medical records, carrying out computerised searches to assist doctors in patient care, dealing with insurance company forms and medicals.

Reception Staff

Reception team do so much more than just answering the phones or booking appointments. They deal with all other enquiries over the telephone and at the reception desk.

Reception duties include:
Arranging home visits and emergency measures
Handling requests test results
Opening and distributing post
Dealing with with repeat prescriptions
